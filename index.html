About

Grex was established in 2015 by industry experts with 40 years in telco and a combined 10 years at NBN.

We are committed to reducing the cost and simplifying the delivery of telecommunications services in Australia.

We work with telecommunications operators, State-owned and publicly listed utilities, Tier 2/3 Retail Service Providers, Non-Telco brands and a variety of other IT&T organisations.

Our key focus is simplifying broadband access & improving commercial terms for our customers.

Above all else, Grex is committed to the success and growth of our customers’ business.

Grex Aggregration

Grex aggregates wholesale broadband and voice products for small and medium-sized telecommunications retail service providers and non-telco companies.

By aggregating demand, Grex is able to provide economies of scale & efficiency benefits that would be inaccessible for individual companies.

Our Aggregated Wholesale Broadband approach provides:

  • Clear and simple costs
  • Scale – lowering the cost of entry and growth
  • Quality – maintained
  • Aligned incentives – Growing your business is our business
  • Intelligence – Analytics and advanced visibility of customer usage and behaviour.

Grex Strategic Sourcing

In a consolidated supply market, purchasers need to define the optimal configuration, investment and focus of the procurement function.

Our considerable years of experience of tackling the issue of purchasing costs has allowed Grex to develop clear strategies for managing the transformation of procurement into a Strategic Sourcing approach.

Grex’s degree of involvement can range from that of facilitator and advisor to the delivery and supply (see Grex Aggregation).

Our approach demonstrates the benefits achieved through a strategic sourcing approach.

Grex Strategic Sourcing:

  • Sets out the immediate and long term viability for your organization of its key IT&T commercial arrangements, and
  • Highlights through the process any immediate contract negotiation opportunities or other quick wins to improve return on investment.
