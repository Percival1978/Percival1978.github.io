Our mission here at In Season Wholefood Cafe is to support healthy active lifestyles.
We believe good food fuels our bodies and fights off disease.


We aim to provide a healthy, wholesome menu with choices for most dietary requirements including Gluten free, Vegan, Vegetarian, refined sugar free and Paleo.


At In Season we aim to source all of our ingredients locally where possible without chemicals or additives and make all of our dishes in house. All this in a friendly atmosphere. We intend to make this a great place to visit and to work making everyone part of our community. We are a family run business who value placing relationships and integrity above all.


We wish to prove healthy food can taste great.
